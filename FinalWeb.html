Quantum Computing

By: Ian Divine

What is Quantum Computing?

Quantum Computing is the use of quantum mechanics to allow for extremely fast processing speeds for problems that need to be solved quick and precise.

With the more powerful processing more can be asked of the computer, so it can be asked much more complex questions than most computers. Anything too complex for your everyday computer to handle, a quantum computer will be able to handle as fast if not faster than your computer. Although they are extremely fast they are not entirely designed for everyday objectives.
