Careers in Quantum Computing

Software Engineer

Quantum Software Engineers are looking at a salary of 60k-200k depending on where they find a workplace. With a salary like that you may think you want to do this job but it is not for the faint of heart, quantum software engineers develop the programs for quantum computers. If you are looking to become a Quantum software engineer you are looking at around 8 years of schooling to master the ideas of quantum physics and linear algrebra as well as quantum programming.

Hardware Engineer

Quantum hardware engineers build everyday objects using quantum technology. You can expect to be in school to obtain a Master's Degree or even a Doctorate in quantum mechanics with a background of computational physics and machine learning. For a salary depending on location of workplace can be as low as 30k for a starting postion and get up to around 140k after you have more experience in the field.

Research Scientist

Quantum research scientists find problems and make scientific assumptions around the idea of quantum theory and conduct experiments to apply their solution until they fix the problem. They have to very familiar with quantum physics to be able to work in this field. You are looking at a Ph.D to maintain a comfotable stay in this feild and need to go through an undergraduate or graduate program for computer science, mathematics, and physics. With a starting salary of right around 30k but through experience can get up to right around 150k.
